Why Start This Blog?

it was really easy for me to fall into ruts when it came to being creative. i got so caught up in trying to make a final product that i was stoked about i didn't even have fun in the process and it made me not want to make art. crafting though, whether by myself or with friends is an outlet that i've grown to really love because it puts a focus on making rather than end product. i wanted to share crafts that i've enjoyed so that other people could try them out too!!
